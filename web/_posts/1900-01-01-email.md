---
title: email
signature: |
    chance.email()
    chance.email({domain: "example.com"})
---

Return a random email with a random domain.

{% highlight js %}
chance.email()
=> 'kawip@piklojzob.gov'
{% endhighlight %}

Optionally specify a domain and the email will be random but the domain will not.

{% highlight js %}
chance.email({domain: 'example.com')
=> 'giigjom@example.com'
{% endhighlight %}
