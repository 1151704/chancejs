---
title: year()
signature: |
    chance.year()
    chance.year({min: 1900, max: 2100})
---

Generate a random year

{% highlight js %}
  chance.year();
  => '2053'
{% endhighlight %}

By default, min is the current year and max is 100 years greater than min.

Optionally specify min, max, or both to limit the range.

{% highlight js %}
  chance.year({min: 1900, max: 2100});
  => '1983'
{% endhighlight %}
