---
title: syllable
signature: |
    chance.syllable()
---

Return a semi-speakable syllable, 2 or 3 letters

{% highlight js %}
chance.syllable();
=> 'fop'
{% endhighlight %}

The syllable is returned in all lower case.

A syllable generally alternates between vowel and consanant and is used as the
core building block for a word.
